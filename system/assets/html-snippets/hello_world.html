Data Transformation Engine

At its core, DUFT supports powerful Python-based data transformation pipelines. Whether you're cleaning data from an OpenMRS instance or merging multiple sources into a central repository, DUFT enables you to write transformations as scripts or Jupyter Notebooks and schedule them as ETL jobs.

These jobs can pull data from local or remote systems, transform it according to business rules, and upload it into a centralised repository or national data warehouse.

Discover Insights and Turn Them Into Knowledge

DUFT makes it easy to build custom dashboards tailored to programme and facility needs. Using modern, industry-grade charting libraries like ApexCharts, dashboards can include filters, search functions, data exports, and layout customisation with minimal setup.

Designed to be deployed both in central servers and at health facility level, DUFT enables data use at the point of care while also supporting strategic-level visualisations for national programmes.
